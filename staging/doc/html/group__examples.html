Detailed Description

The Topology ToolKit - Example programs.

Author
Julien Tierny julie.nosp@m.n.ti.nosp@m.erny@.nosp@m.lip6.nosp@m..fr
Date
October 2017.

Minimalist VTK-based TTK example pipeline, including:

  1. The computation of a persistence curve
  2. The computation of a persistence diagram
  3. The selection of the most persistent pairs of the diagram
  4. The pre-simplification of the data according to this selection
  5. The computation of the Morse-Smale complex on this simplified data
  6. The storage of the output of this pipeline to disk.

This example reproduces the Figure 1 of the TTK companion paper: "The Topology ToolKit", J. Tierny, G. Favelier, J. Levine, C. Gueunet, M. Michaux., IEEE Transactions on Visualization and Computer Graphics, Proc. of IEEE VIS 2017.
